About

Cúrador was founded on the idea that as a society we have been provided with few options for mood-altering consumables that enrich our lives without excessive negative damage to our bodies and minds. For too long, access to indigenous plant medicines that connect us to nature, and each other, have been villainized and criminalized. Since early societies, governing bodies have banished the use of substances that incite free thought, spiritual connectivity, and allow for a journey of self-wellness and awareness.

We recognize the opportunity for growth as a new world is emerging with an openness to try new systems of thought while understanding cultural traditions and rituals that have the power to heighten one's quality of life and positive sense of self.

Our Presence

200+ Dispensaries Across Missouri

Curador brands are available in dispensaries across the state of Missouri. From St. Louis to Springfield, Kansas City to Columbia, our products reach communities statewide, delivering premium cannabis experiences wherever you are.

Born in Missouri, Curador is built on a foundation of quality, education, and consumer well-being. As we continue to grow, our vision extends beyond state lines, expanding thoughtfully to meet the evolving needs of a global cannabis community.

We are committed to increasing access and building meaningful partnerships with dispensaries that share our values, ensuring every product experience reflects the standards we stand behind.
